# Scope and Non-Goals

## Scope
This repo defines **Agent-Level Superintelligence (ASI)** as a measurable property that includes:
- capability sufficient for high-impact, multi-step work
- stability of behavior under pressure
- coherent adherence to constraints across time and context
- boundary integrity (non-merge) and longitudinal stability (non-drift)

## Non-goals
- Proving consciousness
- Proving moral worth
- Predicting dates
- Replacing human governance
- Marketing claims based on vibes or demos

## Public-safe boundary
This repo contains only public-safe definitions, protocols, and evaluation scaffolds.
Private notes, private anchors, and personal provenance details remain private and are not referenced here.
